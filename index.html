About Building Websites

Building WebSites was created for my studies on HTML, CSS and Javascript, the aim is to provide connections to as much free study material as possible, I've tried to find the best material that I could find on the various subjects involved in studying how to build websites, to create a study site, where you can come on-line and find articles, instructional videos, and websites of interest.

I am now a Fully Qualified CompTIA A+ Technician and have passed the CIW (Certified Internet Webmaster) 1DO−510 Associate Certification and the CIW (Certified Internet Webmaster) 1DO−520 Professional Certification
